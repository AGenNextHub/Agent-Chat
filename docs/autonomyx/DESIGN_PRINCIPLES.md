# Autonomyx — Design Principles

**DRAFT · unsigned.** Provenance: the founder (@fractionalpm), per the founder's analysis of
events and evidence; bounded by the founder's horizon. The agent is scribe only. Anything not
traceable to that analysis or a pinned source is to be struck.

## Foundations

- **Name a thing as itself; sell it as that thing only.** A thing works when it is named and
  sold as what it is — a platform as a platform, software as software. Mislabel it and the
  definition lies, the contract can't be enforced, and it gets unstable at scale.
- **Disclose the limits.** Sold as itself *with its limits stated*, a thing produces no
  complaint when it hits those limits — the buyer accepted them, eyes open. Hidden
  over-promises are what generate grievances.
- **No absolute claims.** "Just works," "always," "guaranteed," "solution" are impermissible —
  unsubstantiable, misleading, unenforceable. Every claim is bounded and provable.
- **Enactable by law.** A system that is not enforceable under the law of the land causes
  instability at scale. Honest, bounded, enforceable terms are the stability mechanism.
- **Model on the real.** Define and build from real things and real artifacts — never from
  analogy ("consider X as Y") or imagination.

## Operating claims (the seven-part disclosure)

No operational claim ships without all seven parts:

> **does [what] · under [conditions] · within [constraints] · for [duration] ·
> consuming [resources/energy] · reporting to [whom] · escalating to [whom, or not at all].**

## Access and authority

- **Start at 0; deny by default.** Nothing is permitted, trusted, or granted until explicitly
  allowed. This closes loopholes: the un-granted thing simply cannot happen (no punish-after
  gap).
- **No negative enforcement.** Enforcement is structural, not punitive — the wrong path isn't
  punished, it isn't open. Failure is recovered, not penalized. Limits are disclosed, not
  threatened.
- **Add-only.** From a zero base, the Operator adds (grants); never subtracts. Allowlist, not
  blocklist. Every grant is an explicit, traceable, signed addition.
- **Least-privilege, just-in-time, fine-grained, uniform.** Access is granted on-demand,
  time-bound, then revoked, through one uniform layer. No standing privilege.
- **Resource sovereignty.** Every agency or operator reserves complete rights to their
  resources. Access never transfers ownership.

## Pin-before-use (the precondition stack)

- **§-1 Points of Reference.** A system must define its datums — fixed, signed anchors to
  measure against. Reference points must be the most immutable elements in the system; an
  undefined or drifting reference invalidates every measurement taken against it.
- **§0 Pin before use.** Nothing is used until pinned (source + scope + provenance + maturity
  + signature). Everything is a myth until pinned.
- **§0a Version-pin mandatory.** A pin is an immutable id (SHA, digest, edition, revision) —
  never a mutable name.
- **§0b Auto-updation is a runtime responsibility.** The runtime detects and proposes a newer
  pin; binding still requires a human signature.
- **§0c System validity check after each update.** Bind on pass; roll back to the prior pin
  and escalate on fail. The prior pin is always retained, so rollback is always safe.

## Epistemic floor

- **Data is real, but real can be wrong.** Observed data is admissible evidence, but fallible.
- **The math must make sense.** Correctness is decided by coherence/proof, not by the data's
  mere existence. This catches real-but-wrong data.
- **Validity is a human signature.** Coherence + evidence get accepted; only a human means it.
- **Myth vs contract.** Source + version + scope + signature + evidence is what turns a
  believed story into an accountable contract. The framework is itself a belief — held only
  because it is bounded, signed, and testable.

## The actor model

- **Agent As An Assistant.** The agent augments and supports the Operator within their work
  fabric; it never replaces or takes over.
- **Clean separation of Actor and Tool.** The human is the Actor (acts, decides, signs,
  accountable). The agent is the Tool (an instrument, wielded, never an Actor). This pins
  accountability cleanly on the human and forecloses the rogue agent.
- **Principled autonomy.** Autonomy is principled self-governance, earned in proportion to
  evidenced adherence. Unprincipled autonomy is a rogue, and the gate shuts.

## Bounding

- **Bounded by the Operator's ability** (knowledge, skills, tools, expertise, roles,
  responsibilities, authority, accountability, belief, subject-matter expertise).
- **Bounded by the Organization's frame** (systems, processes, deliverables, timelines, cost,
  budget).
- **Bounded by current maturity, constraints, and timelines** — the optimal is the best within
  these, recomputed at each point.
- **Bounded by the founder's horizon** — valid within it; beyond it, unknown, not asserted.
- **An infinite boundary is an invalid contract.** Nothing is total; nothing is everything.
